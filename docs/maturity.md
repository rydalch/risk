## Maturity Progression

Maturity models typically progress through levels such as:
1. **Initial/Ad Hoc** - Reactive, inconsistent processes
2. **Developing/Repeatable** - Some standardization emerging
3. **Managed/Defined** - Processes documented and followed
4. **Optimized/Proactive** - Continuous improvement
5. **Leading/Risk-Aware** - Advanced, forward-thinking practices
