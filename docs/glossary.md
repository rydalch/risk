# Glossary

Key terms and concepts in cybersecurity risk management and governance.

## Key Risk Concepts

### Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)
Metrics that signal changes in risk levels, often serving as "canaries in coal mines." Associated with threat community changes, loss potential, and control effectiveness.

Reference: https://www.fairinstitute.org/blog/kris-for-cybersecurity-canaries-in-coal-mines

### Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)
Integration of cybersecurity risk into overall business risk management and governance structures.

### Risk Assessment
Systematic process of identifying, analyzing, and quantifying risks to an organization.

### Inherent Risk
Risk before controls are applied. Contrasts with residual risk (risk after controls).

### Cyber Value-at-Risk (CVaR)
Financial metric expressing potential loss from cyber incidents, used to communicate risk to business executives.
