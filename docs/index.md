# Risk Management Knowledge Base

A comprehensive, organized resource hub for cybersecurity risk, governance, and compliance professionals.

## Browse by Topic

- **[Frameworks & Standards](frameworks.md)** - Control frameworks (NIST, CIS, ISO, COBIT) and security standards
- **[Risk Methods](risk-methods.md)** - Quantitative and qualitative risk analysis approaches (FAIR, NIST RMF)
- **[Tools & Libraries](tools.md)** - Risk assessment tools, libraries, and GRC platforms
- **[Threat Modeling](threat-modeling.md)** - Threat analysis, MITRE ATT&CK, and threat assessment resources
- **[Data Analysis](data-analysis.md)** - Data sources, visualization, and Python/R learning resources
- **[Vendor & Supply Chain Risk](vendor-risk.md)** - Third-party and supply chain risk management
- **[Learning Resources](learning.md)** - Training, certifications, books, and courses
- **[Glossary](glossary.md)** - Key terms and concepts
- **[Boardroom & Governance](boardroom.md)** - Board reporting, executive guidance, and governance
- **[Maturity Models](maturity.md)** - CMMI, FISMA, C2M2, and other maturity frameworks
